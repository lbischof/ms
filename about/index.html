About

It's about inspiring you, about making the world more colourful. Colour is life. Colour emerges when light meets darkness. The red yellow seam when light moves over dark and the blue purple seam when dark moves over light. Inspire your imagination and create a colourful life with children when the tactile experience of this renewable resource warms your heart. We carry felting wool in over 100 colours, fibres and locks, 100% wool felt and more. We are proud to be the Canadian Representative for Wizpick Felting Needles.
